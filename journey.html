AI Research & Recovery Journey

2023 - Present | Exploring the evolving AI ecosystem

A New Chapter: From Stroke Recovery to AI Innovation

In 2023, following a life-altering stroke, I discovered AI's transformative potential not just as a technology, but as an assistive tool that became essential to my recovery. Having lost use of my left hand, I could no longer code traditionally. This forced me to adapt by using speech-to-text dictation combined with AI assistance—and unexpectedly made me a better developer. Typing slower meant thinking deeper. Speaking code meant communicating clearer. While I might not be able to type as fast as before, I can definitely write code, correspondence, and communicate with peers far more effectively than ever. This experience revealed accessibility gaps that technology could bridge, and sparked a deep commitment to building solutions that help others. My recovery journey continues today—always recovering, always working—and this ongoing experience keeps me grounded in building technology that truly serves real human needs.

What began as a personal necessity evolved into deep expertise: understanding how AI systems work, their capabilities and limitations, and how they can be leveraged to make workflows more accessible and efficient. With 20+ years of enterprise technology experience and firsthand knowledge of AI as assistive technology, I'm now looking for the right team where I can contribute that combination of experience and perspective.
