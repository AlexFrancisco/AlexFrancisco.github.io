AI Research & Recovery Journey

2023 - Present | Exploring the evolving AI ecosystem

A New Chapter: From Stroke Recovery to AI Innovation

In 2023, following a life-altering stroke, I discovered AI's transformative potential not just as a technology, but as an assistive tool that became essential to my recovery. Unable to speak, AI became my voice—allowing me to communicate proficiently and maintain my professional capabilities during the most challenging period of my life. This experience revealed accessibility gaps that technology could bridge, and sparked a deep commitment to building solutions that help others. My recovery journey continues today—always recovering, always working—and this ongoing experience keeps me grounded in building technology that truly serves real human needs.

What began as a personal necessity evolved into a mission: understanding how AI systems work, their capabilities and limitations, and most importantly, how they can be leveraged to make the world more accessible and efficient—both for individuals facing challenges and enterprises seeking transformation. Today, I bring 20+ years of enterprise technology experience and firsthand knowledge of AI as assistive technology to businesses in the Four Corners region and remotely across the country, building solutions that are both technically robust and genuinely human-centered.
